📌 Как работают DO и DOES

DO → с I, You, We, They

  • I do (я делаю)
  • You do (ты делаешь)
  • We do (мы делаем)
  • They do (они делают)

DOES → с He, She, It

  • He does (он делает)
  • She does (она делает)
  • It does (оно делает)
  • The team does
+ She arranges meetings. / They arrange meetings.
− She doesn't arrange / They don't arrange
? Does she arrange…? / Do they arrange…?
⚠ Главное правило: после DOES/DOESN'T глагол всегда без -s!
❌ Does she arranges? → ✅ Does she arrange?
❌ She doesn't rushes. → ✅ She doesn't rush.
✓Do you have an appointment? — Yes, I do.
✓Does she take a break at noon? — No, she doesn't.
✓What do they prioritise? / What does he prioritise?
✗Does she stays up late? → Does she stay up late?
